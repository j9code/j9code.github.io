30 Day Map Challenge (2023)

The 30 Day Map Challenge is a daily mapping challenge created by Topi Tjukanov. The challenge is open to everyone with the goal of creating a new map each day during November.

Participants create maps based around a different daily theme and post the finished map on social media with the tag #30DayMapChallenge.

This has been on my GIS bucket list for several years and I was able to participate in 2023. Whew!
